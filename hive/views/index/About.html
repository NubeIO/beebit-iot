About BeeBit

BeeBit was founded in 2019 by a group of six students from the Univeristy of Wollongong completing our final year project subject CSIT321. We have been closely collaborating with Nube iO since our formation, on what would eventually become the BeeBit Hive project. BeeBit operates on a philosophy of providing efficient, effective, and usable solutions to business problems through automation and innovative use of information technologies.
